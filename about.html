Our Story

From humble beginnings to cinematic excellence

Founded in 2023, LITECINEMAS has quickly become Sri Lanka's premier cinema destination. What began as a single-screen theater has now blossomed into a nationwide chain of luxury cinemas.

Our mission is simple: to provide movie lovers with an unparalleled cinematic experience through cutting-edge technology, luxurious comfort, and exceptional service.

Today, we operate 15 screens across 5 locations in Sri Lanka, serving over 1 million guests annually.
